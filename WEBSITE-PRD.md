# KanYini Earth Projects Website - PRD

## Overview
Public website for KanYini Earth Projects NGO  
**Tagline:** "One Earth. Many Voices. One Shared Future."

**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks  
**Pages:** 10 core pages  
**Tech:** Next.js 14+, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion

---

## The 10 Pages

1. **Homepage** `/` - Hero, featured content, team, impact (✅ DONE)
2. **About** `/about` - Story, mission/vision/values, team bios
3. **Journal** `/journal` - Article listing
4. **Article Detail** `/journal/[slug]` - Individual article
5. **Books** `/books` - "Dancing with the Universe" showcase
6. **Fellowship** `/fellowship` - Program + application
7. **Media** `/media` - Podcasts & videos
8. **Get Involved** `/get-involved` - Donate, partner, volunteer
9. **Impact** `/impact` - Metrics, stories, reports
10. **Contact** `/contact` - Form, map, FAQ
